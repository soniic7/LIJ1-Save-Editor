Character Status Guide

Click any portrait to cycle states. You can also Click and Drag to paint multiple characters at once!

Locked

Grayscale (0.2 opacity). The character has not been discovered.

In Shop

Translucent (0.4 opacity). Found in the hub world, requires Studs.

Unlocked

Full Color (1.0 opacity). fully purchased and ready for Free Play.
Pro Tip: Left-click cycles forward, and Right-click cycles backward!
